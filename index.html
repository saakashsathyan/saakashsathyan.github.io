About

I'm Saakash Sathyan, an Aerospace Engineering student at UC Irvine graduating June 2027. My focus is mechanical design and system integration — I'm most at home working at the intersection of CAD, fabrication, and cross-disciplinary teams.

I'm currently on the fabrication subteam for SEDS @ UCI's autonomous Mars rover, and serving as Financial Director for AIAA @ UCI — a role that's given me real exposure to program management, sponsorship acquisition, and organizational strategy alongside the engineering work.

I work across the full design cycle: from early concept and CAD through design reviews, material selection, and physical fabrication. I'm a U.S. citizen actively looking for internship and co-op opportunities in aerospace and mechanical engineering.
